Questions

Before you buy

Does this store my actual photos and receipts?

It stores text references — a note pointing to where each photo or receipt lives (your phone's photo album, a cloud drive folder, a PDF). That keeps the tool fast, private and offline. Keep the images themselves in your cloud storage so they survive a loss; the Vault is the organized index that ties them to each item and value.

How is this better than a spreadsheet?

It's purpose-built for claims. It totals your value by room and category, flags underinsurance, and generates a formatted proof-of-ownership report grouped by room with serial numbers and subtotals — ready to hand to an adjuster. A blank spreadsheet does none of that for you.

Where is my data stored? Is it private?

Locally, in your own browser. Nothing is uploaded, there's no account, and it works fully offline. Use Backup to save a copy somewhere safe — ideally off-site or in the cloud, so your record survives the very disaster it's built for.

Is it a subscription?

No. One payment, yours forever. No logins, no monthly fee.

I already own the Home Operations System — do I need this too?

They do different jobs. The Operations System runs your home's systems; the Vault documents your belongings for insurance. Together they cover both sides of protecting your home and what's in it.
